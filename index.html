About Us

Khalid Issa & Associates is a vibrant law firm established in 2020 situated in the suburbs of Karen, within the commercial heart of Nairobi. We advise a wide spectrum of clients in the private sector, institutional and multi-national organisations, foreign investors and governmental institutions.

Our legal professionals practice in English law countries specifically in the fields of banking, finance, immigration, and corporate/commercial transactions. Our other specialist areas include ICT, intellectual property, tech-law, arbitration, tax, energy, project finance, debt raising, risk and compliance services, capital markets, investigations and corporate services.
